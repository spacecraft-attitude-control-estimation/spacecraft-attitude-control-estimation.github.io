PhD Position: Autonomous Cislunar Operations - Funded by Canadian Space Agency

Location: Department of Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering, University of Calgary
Start Date: September 2026
Deadline to Receive Full Consideration: February 16, 2026
We are seeking a highly motivated PhD candidate to join our research group for the 3-year fully funded project on sustainable cislunar operations using autonomous object detection.
This project develops autonomous technologies to help spacecraft safely navigate around the Moon by detecting nearby objects and monitoring surface changes without needing constant contact with Earth. As lunar activity increases, particularly on the far side of the Moon, spacecraft must independently identify artificial/natural objects and surface changes.
