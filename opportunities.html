Opportunities

Undergraduate Students

Interested students (BSc) are encouraged to apply for support through the NSERC USRA or PURE Program. If interested for 2026 call, please reach out to Dr. Cilden-Guler by December 12, 2025.

External Funding

Applicants who hold a fellowship or other funding will receive strong consideration, even if no positions are currently available. If you have a fellowship or scholarship and are interested in joining the SPACE Lab, please email your CV, transcript, and cover letter to Dr. Cilden-Guler. Use the subject line: ‘Application for position with external fellowship [your name-surname]’.

Post-Graduate and Post-Doctoral Research Scholarships

Applicants who would like to apply for a scholarship/fellowship and are interested in joining the SPACE Lab, please email your CV, transcript, and cover letter to Dr. Cilden-Guler. Use the subject line: ‘Application for position through MSc/PhD/Postdoc scholarship [your name-surname]’.

Please explore the Faculty of Graduate Studies' website. Some of the scholarship opportunities: NSERC Postgraduate and Postdoctoral Programs, Alberta Innovates Graduate Student Scholarships etc.
